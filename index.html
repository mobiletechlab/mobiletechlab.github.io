The Mobile Tech Lab (MOTEL)

About the lab

The mission of the Mobile Technology Laboratory (MoTeL) at Westerdals Oslo ACT, Faculty of Technology, is to design, develop and deploy innovative and value-creating applications and services in individual and organizational contexts and evaluate their adoption, use, and impact.

MOTEL’s vision is to co-create scientific knowledge and value through innovative applications of information and communication technologies by engaging with multiple stakeholders in the international society and local community.

MOTEL seeks to conduct trans-disciplinary applied research at the intersections of computer science, human computer interaction, software engineering, and social and behavioral sciences with a focus on HCI and mobile applications and services for individuals and organizations. MOTEL’s applied research projects will be theoretically informed by its foundational research program on understanding socio-technical systems and interactions in terms of human-technical affordances and technological intersubjectivity. MOTEL seeks to conduct research in close collaboration with industry partners from both private and public sector, in a variety of areas such as commerce, health, interface design, education and communication.
